# 🦥 Hoji's Honor Guild

> *Every Adventurer walks the path. Keepers heed the call — and help you finish yours.*

## What Is Hoji's Honor Guild?

Hoji's Honor Guild is a shame-proof, community-driven accountability app 
for anyone who has ever meant it when they made a commitment but still 
couldn't follow through.

No shame. No streaks. No hustle culture.

Just honest commitment, a Guild that shows up with you, and a sloth named 
Hoji who has been on this branch the whole time.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Sometimes the missing piece isn't a better system — it's a witness. 
Someone who sees you commit, holds the clock, and shows up when you 
go quiet. Not to judge. Not to shame. Just to hold the torch until 
you reach the finish.

That's what Hoji's Honor Guild is built to do.

---

## The Two Roles

**Adventurer**
Every member is an Adventurer. Build your quests, set your deadlines, 
define what done looks like, and start the clock. The Guild is with 
you every step of the way.

**Keeper**
Not everyone answers the call. Keepers voluntarily witness and verify 
Adventurer commitments. They don't judge. They don't coach. They hold 
the torch. First to accept owns the verification. Keeper Hours toggle 
prevents burnout.

It's enough to be on this journey. If you ever feel called to walk 
alongside someone else — the Guild welcomes that too.

---

## How It Works

1. Create a quest — any commitment worth keeping
2. Set a deadline — no exceptions, the clock is real
3. Define what done looks like before you begin
4. Decide how often — once, recurring, or anywhere in between
5. Start the clock — the Guild is with you
6. Escalating reminders fire automatically as your deadline approaches
7. Submit your proof when complete
8. Your Keeper verifies — done or not done, no in between
9. Chose not to finish? Consciously close it — 
   honesty builds ownership muscle

Every quest is yours to define. The Guild resets with you every time.

---

## The Keeper Shout Out

After a set number of unanswered reminders the system broadcasts to 
available Keepers — *"Adventurer in need!"*

First Keeper to respond owns it. No Adventurer goes quiet alone.
